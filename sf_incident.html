San Francisco Incident Analysis Project (Work in Progress)

Mapping and Analyzing San Francisco Incident Data
Python, MySQL, Tableau

PROJECT DASHBOARD

GOAL

Improve understanding of San Francisco and increase public safety by identifying high-risk areas and times through data analysis, and provide recommendations for reducing incident rates.

QUESTIONS TO ANSWER

1. Hotspot analysis:
       1. Where are the hotspots of incidents located?
       2. Are there any geographic patterns in the distribution of incidents?
       3. Which areas have the highest incidence of crime?
       4. Are there any factors that contribute to the clustering of incidents in certain areas?
2. Time analysis:
       1. Are there any temporal patterns in the frequency of incidents (e.g. monthly, weekly, hourly)?
       2. Are there any seasonal trends in the frequency of incidents?
       3. Is there a specific day or time when incidents are more likely to occur?
       4. Has there been an increase or decrease in the frequency of incidents over time?
3. Incident category analysis:
       1. What are the most common types of incidents reported?
       2. Are certain types of incidents more likely to occur in certain areas or at certain times?
